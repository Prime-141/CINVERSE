About the movie

Welcome to the world of Minecraft, where creativity isn`t just a tool-it`s key to survival! Four misfits find themselves grappling with everyday challenges when they are suddenly pulled through a mysterious portal into the Overworld-a bizarre, cubic wonderland fueled by imagination. To return home, they must navigate this unfamiliar realm, master its mechanics, and fend off threats like Piglins and Zombies. Guided by an unexpected expert crafter, their journey will test their courage and push them to rediscover the unique creativity that will help them thrive. Not just in this world, but in their own.

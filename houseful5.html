About the movie :

Housefull 5 is an upcoming Indian comedy film directed by Tarun Mansukhani and produced by Sajid Nadiadwala under his production banner, Nadiadwala Grandson Entertainment. It marks the fifth installment of the Housefull franchise.[1] The film features an ensemble cast including Akshay Kumar, Riteish Deshmukh, Abhishek Bachchan, Sanjay Dutt, Fardeen Khan, Nana Patekar, Jackie Shroff, Dino Morea, Jacqueline Fernandez, Nargis Fakhri, Chitrangada Singh, Sonam Bajwa, Soundarya Sharma, and Chunky Pandey.
